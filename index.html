Welcome to Basic Career Mentor, where we provide coaching for English subject for various competitive exams. Our institute is headed by Neeraj Ojha sir, who has been providing coaching for the past 17 years and has helped numerous students achieve the highest marks in English paper.
At Basic Career Mentor, we cover the subject in its entirety, including English grammar, vocabulary development, and comprehension skills. Our coaching is tailored to suit the needs of each student, whether they are a beginner or an advanced learner. We offer coaching for various competitive exams, including SSC-CGL, CPO, CHSL, STENO, MTS, DSSSB-PRT, TGT, PGT, and more.
Our coaching methodology is designed to provide students with a solid foundation in English and equip them with the skills and knowledge they need to excel in their exams. We have a team of experienced and qualified teachers who use a combination of lectures, interactive sessions, and practice exercises to ensure that students are well-prepared for their exams.


We also provide regular assessments and feedback to help students track their progress and identify areas where they need to improve. Our aim is to provide our students with the best possible coaching experience and help them achieve their career goals.
